Leave and Time Off

Employees receive 24 paid leave days annually. The breakdown and guidelines are outlined below:

Leave Type Annual Allowance Guidelines & Requirements
Casual Leave 12 Days Designed for short-term personal needs. Requires advance approval from the manager.
Sick Leave 6 Days For medical emergencies and illnesses. May require medical documentation after three consecutive days.
Privilege Leave 6 Days Planned holidays and longer time off. Must be requested and approved in advance.

Sick Leave Rule

Sick leave may require medical documentation (doctor certificate) if taken for more than three consecutive days.

The leave and time off policy applies across all departments unless a local legal requirement specifies a different approach. Managers are responsible for communicating expectations related to leave and time off and ensuring team compliance.
